**Requirement Description**: Policies on risk analysis and information system security covering the cybersecurity risks faced by each entity, taking into account sector-specific elements
**Objective Utilities**: Establishes systematic approach to cybersecurity risk identification, assessment, and management appropriate to the entity's sector and risk profile
**Audit Question**: Are cybersecurity risk analysis policies documented and do they address sector-specific cybersecurity risks?

**Audit Question**: Are incident handling procedures documented covering detection, response, and recovery phases of cybersecurity incidents?

**Chapter Title: Reporting Obligations**
**Objective Title**: NIS2 Article 23.1 - Early Warning (24-hour notification)
**Requirement Description**: Submit early warning without undue delay and in any event within 24 hours of becoming aware of the significant cybersecurity incident
**Objective Utilities**: Enables rapid coordination and response by national authorities to contain incident impact across sectors
**Audit Question**: Can the entity identify significant cybersecurity incidents and report them to competent authorities within 24 hours?
**Conformity Question**: Are incident reporting procedures established with clear criteria for determining reporting requirements?

**Chapter Title: Reporting Obligations**
**Objective Title**: NIS2 Article 23.2 - Incident Report (within one month)
**Requirement Description**: Submit incident report without undue delay and in any event within one month of becoming aware of the significant cybersecurity incident
**Objective Utilities**: Provides detailed incident information to support regulatory oversight and sector-wide threat intelligence
**Audit Question**: Are detailed incident reports prepared and submitted within one month containing required information elements?
**Conformity Question**: Do incident reports provide sufficient detail to support regulatory assessment and sector threat analysis?

**Chapter Title: Reporting Obligations**

**Objective Utilities**: Captures comprehensive incident analysis and improvement measures for regulatory review and sector learning
**Audit Question**: Can the entity provide comprehensive final reports upon request including incident details and response effectiveness?
